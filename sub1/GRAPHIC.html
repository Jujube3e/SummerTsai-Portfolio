Product Design

This product redesign project allowed me to creatively come up with a way to showcase this childhood treat in a new way. I started brainstorming by carefully observing the original packaging, taking notes on the design choices and color. The Japanese word “Botan” means peony so I wanted to design around that. I initially wanted to showcase the unique prize stickers that are found in each box by creating a messy, collage print. In the end it looked too busy and crowded so I went with mainly incorporating peonies.

In this project we designed a coat of arms that represented our culture. My goal was to create a piece rich with symbolism that could be read on multiple levels- from broad cultural motifs to more personal meanings. In my rough drafts I aimed to incorporate drawings that had deep meaning to create a cohesive emblem. I tried incorporating both Taiwanese and Chinese objects and symbols into my drafts representing my heritage and traditions. Some symbolic ideas I explored included plum blossoms, chinese words, national animals, and asian art patterns.
